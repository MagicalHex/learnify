# Learnify

**Learnify** is an AI-powered learning management app that lets you create your own courses and track your learning progress efficiently. It combines AI-generated roadmaps with flexible time tracking so you can optimize your learning experience.

---

## Features

- **Register & Login**: Create an account to start building your courses.  
- **Create AI Roadmaps**: Copy and paste the provided prompt (replace `[subject]` with your chosen topic) into your preferred AI (LLM) tool.  
- **Import AI Response**: Take the roadmap generated by the AI and paste it into Learnify to start your course.  
- **Time Tracking**:  
  - Start a timer for each step.  
  - Optionally add manual time for better learning optimization.  
  - Track your learning speed and adjust pacing accordingly.  
- **Step Completion**: Mark each step as completed to see your progress.  
- **Custom Courses**: Finish your own courses created with AI, and celebrate your achievement!

---

## How to Use

1. **Register/Login** to your Learnify account.  
2. Use the prompt template:  
3. Paste the AI-generated roadmap into Learnify.  
4. Start working through the steps:  
- Click **Start Timer** when you begin a step.  
- Click **Add Manual Time** if you worked offline or need to adjust time spent.  
5. Complete steps one by one.  
6. Celebrate when your course is finished!

## Tech Stack

- Frontend: React + TypeScript  
- Backend: Node.js + Express  
- Database: MongoDB  
- AI: LLM (external) for roadmap generation
